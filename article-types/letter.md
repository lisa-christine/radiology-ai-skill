# Letter to Editor

## Limits
| Element | Limit |
|---------|-------|
| Words | 350 |
| Abstract | None |
| References | 5 |
| Figures | 0 |
| Tables | 0 |

**CLAIM**: Not required

---

## Purpose
Constructive scientific comments on recently published articles.

---

## Format

```
Dear Editor,

We read with interest the article by [Author] et al. titled "[Title]" published in [Month Year] [1].

[Specific scientific comment with rationale.]

[Additional comment if applicable.]

[Constructive suggestion or clarification request.]

[Signatures]
```

---

## Process
1. Letter sent to original authors for response
2. Both may be published together
3. Must be signed with contact info

## Guidelines
- Be constructive and specific
- Focus on scientific content
- Avoid personal criticism
- Don't repeat points already addressed in article
